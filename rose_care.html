Rose Care Guide

Basic Rose Care

  • Watering: Regular watering is crucial, but avoid over-watering. Ensure good drainage to prevent root rot.

  • Sunlight: Most roses thrive in full sun (6-8 hours a day).

  • Soil: Use well-drained soil enriched with organic matter.

  • Pruning: Regular pruning helps maintain shape and encourages blooming.

Seasonal Care

  • Spring: Time to plant new roses and start regular maintenance.

  • Summer: Keep up with watering and watch for pests.

  • Fall: Prepare roses for winter by mulching and cutting back.

  • Winter: Protect roses from frost and cold weather.

Troubleshooting

  • Pests: Common pests include aphids and spider mites. Use appropriate treatments to manage infestations.

  • Diseases: Watch for signs of fungal diseases like black spot and powdery mildew. Apply fungicides as needed.
